Unique Benefits

Individualized

The mobile view guides each athlete through their personal workout based on their history and ability that day. No more under or over training as each athlete is set at their optimal level all the time.

Real Time

Each set is adjusted in real time allowing athletes to get the most out of every workout, every time. Coaches receive instant feedback on the progress of their athletes throughout any given workout and over time.

Any Time

Athletes access and follow their workouts at any time, while coaches monitor their team's activity and progress in person or remotely.

Seamless

Our mobile and web tools are integrated for maximum tracking and analysis leading to better athletes and more informed coaches.

Motivational

Athletes more fully engage in their training because now they see the difference their effort is making over time, which drives them to work even harder than before.

Accountable

Coaches know the work ethic and progress of all their players, allowing more time for actual coaching and requiring less for babysitting.
